# 🚀 R.O.T.O.
**A Physics-Based Modular Ship Building & Exploration Experience.**

Inspired by the mechanics of *Worlds Adrift*, **ROTO** (Reconnaissance Operations & Terrestrial Observer), is a technical exploration into high-fidelity, physics-driven gameplay in the Godot Engine. Build your ship piece-by-piece, customize your ROTO-BOT, and navigate a world of floating islands and dynamic wind walls.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛠️ Modular Ship Building
Construction is more than just clicking a prefab. In ROTO, you build **frames** and **panels** individually, creating a structural skeleton that reacts to physics, weight, and damage. 
- **13+ Ship Components**: From thrusters and stabilizers to sails and turrets.
- **Physics Flight**: Every propeller, wing, and engine contributes to the actual lift and drag of your vessel.

### 🤖 ROTO-BOT Customization
Your identity in the sky. Customize your ROTO-BOT with:
- **9 Metal Origins**: Each with unique stat modifiers.
- **Dynamic Face Emotes**: Express yourself with animated facial systems.
- **Deep Visual Customization**: Swap parts and colors to make your bot unique.

### 🗺️ Island Exploration & Creation
- **Procedural World**: Explore Haven and the Badlands across 12 unique biomes.
- **Island Creator**: Use our standalone editor to sculpt terrain using Marching Cubes and SDF technology.
- **Community Hub**: Share and discover islands created by other players.
