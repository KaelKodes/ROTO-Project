# 🚀 ROTO: Beyond the Skies

**A Physics-Based Modular Ship Building & Exploration MMO.**

Inspired by the mechanics of *Worlds Adrift*, **ROTO** is a multiplayer sandbox built in the Godot Engine. Build your ship piece-by-piece, outfit it with weapons and components, craft with quality-driven materials, and navigate a sky world of floating islands, tiered zones, and dynamic wind walls — all as a customizable ROTO-BOT.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛠️ Modular Ship Building
Build ships from the skeleton up — every frame, panel, and component is placed individually and reacts to real physics.
- **35+ Ship Components**: Thrusters, propellers, sails, stabilizers, generators, shields, turrets, cannons, lasers, bombers, storage, respawn beacons, balloons, and more.
- **12 Weapon Types**: 6 turret variants and 6 stationary mounts, each with distinct FBX models and metal-colored materials.
- **Physics Flight**: Every propeller, wing, and engine contributes to actual lift, drag, and thrust.
- **Wood & Metal Construction**: Build frames and panels from oak, pine, maple, or deadwood — or forge them from any of the 9 craftable metals.

### ⚒️ Crafting & Quality System
- **Quality Blending**: Resources carry quality ratings (Q1–Q10). Mining blends quality via weighted average across your entire stack — build smarter, not just more.
- **Tiered Crafting**: Craft components at stations with multi-slot metal recipes. Higher quality materials produce superior parts.

### 🤖 ROTO-BOT Customization
Your identity in the sky. Customize your ROTO-BOT with:
- **9 Metal Origins**: Iron, Copper, Silver, Aluminium, Nickel, Gold, Titanium, Zinc, Tungsten — each with unique stat modifiers (hull, speed, carry weight, power, resistance).
- **Animated Face Emotes**: 6 expressable emotions with auto-revert and network broadcast.
- **Deep Visual Customization**: Button styles, screen colors, antenna styles, age weathering, and arm height.

### 🗺️ World & Exploration
- **Tiered Zone System**: Navigate outward through Haven → Frontier → Badlands → Abyss, each with escalating danger and rarer resources.
- **Wind Walls**: Cross dynamic barriers between zones to access higher-tier islands and metals.
- **Drifting Islands**: Islands orbit on deterministic drift paths synchronized between client and server.
- **Grapple & Mine**: Latch onto resources and nodes with a physics-based grapple beam, reel in, and mine or gather.

### 🏝️ Island Creator
- **Standalone Editor**: Sculpt terrain using Marching Cubes and SDF technology — ellipsoids, noise displacement, boolean operations.
- **Community Hub**: Upload islands to the server, browse and vote on community creations, leave comments.
- **Live Integration**: Top-voted community islands appear in the main menu background.

### 🌐 Multiplayer
- **Dedicated Servers**: Authoritative server with client prediction and net-state synchronization.
- **Live Chat**: In-game chat panel with message history.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Remote server monitoring with CPU, RAM, network, and zone strain metrics.
